World Wars

Global conflicts that reshaped modern history

The World Wars Era was a time of massive global conflict that caused destruction but also led to major technological progress and reshaped the modern world.

Freedom Movements

Struggles for independence and democracy

Freedom movements were struggles by countries and people to gain independence from foreign or colonial rule. These movements happened across Asia, Africa, and other parts of the world, especially during the 18th to 20th centuries.

They were driven by the desire for self-rule, equality, and national identity.

Modern Era

Technology, AI and space exploration age

The Modern Era is the current phase of human history, where the world is rapidly changing due to science, technology, and global communication. It is often called the age of innovation because new discoveries and inventions are transforming everyday life.

In this era, technology plays a major role. Computers, smartphones, the internet, and artificial intelligence have made life faster and more connected.
